About UniversityPYQ

Your trusted platform for previous year question papers

Our Mission

UniversityPYQ is dedicated to making quality educational resources accessible to every student. We believe that previous year question papers are invaluable study materials that help students understand exam patterns, practice effectively, and achieve academic success.

How It Works

1. Sign Up

Create your free account to get started

2. Upload & Share

Upload your question papers to help others

3. Download & Study

Access thousands of papers for your studies

Features

  • Free access to all papers
  • Advanced search and filtering
  • Bookmark your favorite papers
  • Upload and share resources
  • Quality control through admin review
  • Mobile-friendly design
  • Secure file storage
  • Community-driven content

Quality Assurance

All uploaded papers go through a review process by our administrators to ensure:

  • Academic authenticity and relevance
  • Proper categorization and metadata
  • File quality and readability
  • Compliance with educational fair use policies
